HoneyBurn Ingredients

Inside every drop of "HoneyBurn" you'll find:

100% natural and unique ingredients that are clinically proven to support the support healthy weight loss

1

Wild Raspberry

Wild raspberry, also known as Rubus idaeus, contains a natural compound called raspberry ketones. Raspberry ketones have been shown to increase the secretion of adiponectin, a hormone that regulates glucose levels and fatty acid breakdown. This increase in adiponectin levels leads to an increase in the metabolism of fat cells, resulting in fat burning.

A meta-analysis published in the International Journal of Obesity examined the effects of raspberry ketones on weight loss in humans. The study found that raspberry ketones significantly increased weight loss and reduced body fat percentage compared to a placebo group.
2

Kudzu

Kudzu is an ancient Chinese medicinal herb derived from root extract or powder from Pueraria lobata plant roots. It belongs to the genus Pueraria in the Leguminosae family and has been traditionally used for healing purposes since 1694 BC.

The active compounds within Kudzu are known as daidzin and puerarin, which are thought to be responsible for the herb's antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, metabolic modulation, cardiovascular protection, and neuroprotection properties.

Human clinical trials have provided mixed results regarding Kudzu’s potential effectiveness in promoting weight loss, but animal trials suggest some potential merit: A 2018 study reported that rats given kudzu root starch had lower belly fat concentrations than placebo-treated controls, while another study using mice found lower body mass index (BMI) scores when supplemented with daidzin derived from kudzu root.
3

Olive Leaves

Olive leaves contain a compound called oleuropein, which has been shown to have numerous health benefits, including weight loss. Oleuropein works by activating a specific receptor in the body called the PPAR receptor. This receptor plays a crucial role in regulating metabolism and fat storage. When oleuropein activates the PPAR receptor, it triggers a series of events that promote fat burning and reduces appetite.

Moreover, oleuropein promotes fat burning by increasing the production of a hormone called adiponectin.
4

Bee Pollen

Bee pollen contains compounds that may help to promote fat burning, particularly in stubborn areas of the body such as the belly and thighs. These compounds are called flavonoids, which are antioxidants that have been shown to have anti-obesity effects.

Moreover, bee pollen has been shown to have anti-inflammatory properties, which can be beneficial for weight loss.
5

Sodium Alginate

When sodium alginate is consumed, it forms a gel-like substance in the stomach that can help to increase feelings of fullness and reduce hunger. This is due to the gel's ability to delay stomach emptying, which might make you feel fuller for longer periods of time.

A recent study investigated the effects of sodium alginate on weight loss in overweight and obese individuals. The study found that participants who consumed sodium alginate supplements experienced significant reductions in body weight, body fat percentage, and waist circumference compared to those who received a placebo.
6

Raw Wildflower Honey

Raw wildflower honey is a type of honey that is produced by bees that collect nectar from a variety of wildflowers. Unlike processed honey, which is heated and filtered, raw wildflower honey retains all of its natural enzymes, antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals.

The core mechanisms behind the weight loss and fat-burning properties of raw wildflower honey are due to its unique composition of natural sugars, antioxidants, and anti-inflammatory compounds. The natural sugars provide energy without causing a rapid spike in blood sugar levels, while the antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds help to reduce inflammation.
7

Royal Jelly

Royal jelly has been shown to promote weight loss through several mechanisms. Firstly, it contains a high concentration of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter that regulates appetite and satiety. By increasing acetylcholine levels, royal jelly can help to reduce food intake and promote feelings of fullness.

Secondly, royal jelly contains a unique protein called royalactin, which has been shown to increase energy expenditure and fat oxidation in the body. This means that the body burns more calories and fat, leading to weight loss.
